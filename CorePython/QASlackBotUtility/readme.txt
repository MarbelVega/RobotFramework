The tokens for this service will not be stored in source control for security issues.

To retreive a token, click on the name of the slack channel in the slack app (CosentialDev in our case),
Choose Administration, Choose Manage Apps.

A webpage will open, and from there you can click on Custom Integrations on the left navigation.
Choose Bots in the middle.
Choose Configuration in the middle navigation link on the Bots section.

Here are our existing integrations, and you can use a key from those.

If you want to set a new key up, use Add to Slack, fill in the username and other required info, then click Add Bot Information.
